Register Now for ICBO 202x

* Student fee (discount coupon): To request a student fee discount, please submit a request to mail{at}domain.com by Month dayth, attaching some document that proves your student's relationship with the educational institution. You should ask for that only if you are registered in a degree program (undergraduate or graduate) at an accredited educational institution at the time of your submission.

** Different prices for developing countries: Less developed countries are defined here as countries with a human development index of <0.8 (see List of countries by Human Development Index).

The in-person registration fee covers:

  • Coffee-break and refreshments every day (From Month dayth to Month dayst).
  • Lunch every day (From Month dayth to Month dayst).
  • One group dinner (TBD).
  • Access to the poster session and welcome reception (non alcoholic drinks) on Month dayrd during posters and software demo session.
